About Masashi Kishimoto

Kishimoto was born on November 8 1974 in the Okayama prefecture as the oldest identical twin of Seishii Kishimoto. He took a massive intrest in Akira Toriyama's Dragon Ball. Kishimoto went on to make the most popular manga worldwide called Naruto. Naruto was a massive hit in japan but also in America.

About Takehiko Inoue

Takehiko Inoue was born in Kagoshima Japan in the year 1967. As a kid in high school he stayed to his studied and his love for basketball. At the time he hadnt even decided to become a Managka yet. His temas results werent amazing but he saus ot helped him grow into a better person. Inoue has three absolutes in his life which are his unwavering love for cats, his passion for storytelling and srtwork, and of course his love for basketball.This lead him to drop out of university and chase after being a Mangaka. Takehiko inoue made one of the greatest Sports Manga of all time, which of course was about Basketball. This Manga was called Slam Dunk. He also made another really popular manga about a Swordsman named Miyamoto Musashi Called Vagabond. Below you will see a phto of some of his art work.

About Sui Ishida

Sui Ishida was born in Fukuoka on December 28 1986. Not much is known about his life before the publishing of Tokyo Ghoul. He stated on social media that as a child, rather than reading he was "constantly swinging on horizontal bars", which means he was very likely athletic. He also worked as an tattoo artist before being a mangaka.Ishida created the manga Tokyo Ghoul Which was a hit around the world for its goregous art and gripping story.
